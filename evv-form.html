When to use this form
Submit a correction only when a technical problem prevented you from completing your normal EVV check-in or check-out through WellSky at the time of the visit. If you are unsure whether your situation qualifies, contact the office before submitting.
⚠️ Important — Do not rely on this form
Manual corrections are reviewed by the office and flagged in WellSky. Frequent submissions may affect your standing and could trigger a compliance review. Missouri Medicaid requires EVV to be completed electronically at the time of the visit. This form is a last resort for genuine technical failures — not a backup for forgetting to clock in or out. If you are having repeated EVV issues, contact the office before your next shift.
